When we move from one place to another the measure of total length we cover is called the distance. The distance is measured in meters and is denoted as lowercase M.
Because direction isspecified displacement becomes a vector. The time you took for The Detour was greater than the time you could go straight across but if you call someone to tell them where you are in both the cases your location is this town which is 20 km north of your home.
If the distance traveled is traveled in a straight line the displacement will be the same magnitude as the distance. In most cases in our day-to-day lives the distance covered is greater than the displacement.
